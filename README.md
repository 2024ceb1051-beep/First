## Problem Statement

This dashboard helps energy providers, government agencies, environmental analysts, and business stakeholders understand global energy consumption patterns across different countries, regions, and renewable energy sources. It provides valuable insights into electricity consumption measured in Kilowatt Hours (KWH) and Consumption Units (CSU), enabling decision-makers to monitor energy usage, compare regional performance, and evaluate the contribution of various renewable energy sources.

The dashboard consolidates multiple energy-related indicators into a single interactive Tableau dashboard, allowing users to analyze energy consumption from different geographical and operational perspectives. Through interactive charts and visual comparisons, users can evaluate country-wise electricity consumption, compare regional energy demand, and analyze renewable energy production across multiple energy sources.

By monitoring energy utilization trends and comparing renewable energy contributions across different regions, organizations can identify high energy consumption areas, optimize resource allocation, support sustainable energy planning, and make informed policy decisions. The dashboard serves as a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution for transforming raw energy data into meaningful and actionable insights.
